Thesis Research · 2025

Impact of Thermal Processing Methods on Cassava (Abrabopa)

A comprehensive investigation into how solar, oven, and fermented drying techniques affect the pasting properties of cassava flour, measured by Rapid Visco Analyser (RVA). Findings revealed that solar drying best preserves native granule structure while fermentation produces the most shear-sensitive starch.
